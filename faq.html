Questions & answers

How MMG works, in plain terms.

A quick guide to who we serve, what we offer, and what to expect when you reach out. Have a question that isn't here? Talk with Andrew.

Who does MMG serve?

MMG primarily works with personal injury attorneys, medical providers, and trusted industry vendors across Florida, with a strong focus on South Florida.

What does MMG offer?

MMG offers ongoing marketing partnerships, event sponsorship opportunities, and curated professional networking events.

What happens during a consultation?

Andrew will learn about your business, the people you want to reach, and your goals. He will then recommend the most appropriate marketing or sponsorship next step.

How do I register for an event?

Event registration is handled through Eventbrite. Each upcoming event page links directly to the official registration page.

Can I sponsor an event?

Yes. Sponsorship availability depends on the event and the audience. Contact Andrew to discuss the right opportunity for your brand.

Who attends MMG events?

Events are designed primarily for personal injury attorneys, medical providers, and trusted industry partners. Each event page identifies its intended audience.

Does MMG guarantee referrals, cases, or clients?

No. MMG creates marketing, sponsorship, and networking opportunities. Participation does not guarantee referrals, clients, cases, or business outcomes.

Is MMG a law firm or medical provider?

No. MMG is not a law firm or medical provider and does not provide legal or medical advice.

Still have a question?

Tell Andrew what you're exploring and he'll follow up to recommend the right next step.
