Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information Miller's Marketing Group collects, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Miller's Marketing Group ("MMG," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you visit our website, submit a consultation request, subscribe to updates, or interact with us in connection with our events and services.

Information we collect

Information you provide

When you submit a consultation request or otherwise contact us, we may collect your name, company, professional role, email address, phone number, area of interest, and any message you choose to send. If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect the email address you provide.

Information collected automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect limited technical information such as your browser type, device, general location, pages viewed, and how you arrived at the site. This information helps us understand how the site is used and improve it.

Event registration

Registration for MMG events is handled through Eventbrite. Information you provide to register is collected and processed by Eventbrite under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review Eventbrite's policy before registering.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiries and schedule consultations.
  • To provide and improve our marketing, sponsorship, and event services.
  • To send updates about upcoming events when you have asked to receive them.
  • To understand and improve how our website performs.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate our website and communicate with you — for example, event registration (Eventbrite), scheduling, email and newsletter delivery, and website analytics. These providers may only use your information to perform services on our behalf. We may also disclose information when required by law or to protect the safety, rights, or property of MMG and others.

Cookies and analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences and measure site usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features of the site.

Your choices

  • You may unsubscribe from our emails at any time using the link in any message.
  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you by contacting us using the details below.

Data retention

We retain personal information for as long as needed to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, to maintain our business relationship, or as required by law.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information we collect. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's privacy

Our website and services are intended for professionals and are not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of the website after changes are posted indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact us

Questions about this policy or your information can be sent to contact@millersmarketinggroup.com, by phone at 561-888-9450, or by mail to Miller's Marketing Group, Plantation, Florida.
