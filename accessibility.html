Accessibility Statement

MMG wants everyone to feel welcome — online and in the room. Here's our commitment and how to reach us if you need assistance.

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Our commitment

Miller's Marketing Group is committed to making our website and events accessible to as many people as possible, including people with disabilities. We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA as our standard and to continually improve the experience for all visitors.

What we do

  • Use clear headings and a logical, consistent page structure.
  • Support keyboard navigation and visible focus indicators.
  • Aim for sufficient color contrast between text and backgrounds.
  • Provide descriptive text alternatives for meaningful images.
  • Respect reduced-motion preferences for animations and transitions.
  • Design for use across phones, tablets, and desktop screens.

Known limitations

We are actively improving the site, and some content may not yet fully meet our accessibility goals — for example, certain third-party content (such as embedded registration or video) may rely on external providers. Where a barrier exists, we will do our best to provide the information or service you need through another method.

Accessibility at our events

We want our events to be welcoming to everyone. If you need an accommodation to attend or participate in an MMG event, please contact us ahead of time and we will do our best to assist.

Need help or want to share feedback?

If you have trouble accessing any part of this website, or if you have suggestions for how we can improve, please let us know. We welcome your feedback and will respond as quickly as we can.
